📖 How it works

  1. Mark an order as Processing, then Shipped in Orders page.
  2. When marked Shipped, backend auto-creates a GHN shipping order and saves the tracking code.
  3. Click ✅ Delivered above to simulate GHN calling our webhook with status delivered.
  4. Order auto-updates to Delivered, stock logs are written, and customer gets a notification.
  5. ↩ Return / ❌ Cancel simulate hoàn hàng — order becomes Cancelled and stock is restocked.

In production: Register /api/v1/shipping/ghn-webhook in the GHN Merchant Portal and set GHN_API_KEY + GHN_SHOP_ID in your environment.
