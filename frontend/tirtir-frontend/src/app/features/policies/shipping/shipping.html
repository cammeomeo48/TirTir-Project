Shipping Policy

As soon as your order is confirmed, changes once the order is placed. Most orders are packed and shipped within 1–3 business days. For international orders, shipping times may vary a bit by region, but usually take 3–7 business days. Please keep in mind that delivery might take a little longer during storewide sales or new product launches because of high order volumes. Sometimes, unexpected events like weather, carrier disruptions, customs duties, or local inspections can also cause delays.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, TIRTIR shall not be liable for any delays, losses, or damages arising from or related to shipping delays, customs duties, or interruptions caused by unforeseen circumstances, including but not limited to, weather, carrier disruptions, customs inspections, or any other events beyond our reasonable control. International customers are responsible for any duties, taxes, and fees imposed by their government.

To help ensure your order arrives smoothly, please provide the correct and complete shipping address when placing your order. We kindly ask that you enter your full legal name—not a nickname or abbreviation—as this helps prevent any delivery issues. To avoid recognition errors in our shipping system, please enter all addresses in English only. For our customers in Arabic-speaking regions, where postal codes may not be applicable, we encourage you to fill out your address as detailed and accurately as possible to help us deliver your order without complications.

Incorrect Delivery Information / Inability to Contact the Recipient

If your package cannot be delivered due to an incorrect or incomplete address, or if the delivery service cannot contact the recipient, we are unfortunately unable to reship or refund the order. To help ensure a smooth delivery, we kindly recommend providing complete and accurate shipping information (phone number, email, name, building, room, entrance code, etc.) and keeping an eye on your phone and email for any messages from the delivery service. If you have any issues with your package, please feel free to contact your local post office or courier right away, and we will do our best to assist.

We strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible, but sometimes delivery may be delayed or interrupted due to circumstances beyond our control, such as inclement weather, service disruptions, customs duties, local customs inspections, or other unforeseen events. TIRTIR is unable to take responsibility for any loss, damage, or expense arising from such delays or interruptions, and cannot issue refunds for shipping fees or the order total in these cases.

Hazardous Goods

Each country has its own policies regarding hazardous materials. While we are unable to provide specific information about these regulations, we recommend contacting your local customs office to determine if the product you are purchasing is classified as hazardous.
